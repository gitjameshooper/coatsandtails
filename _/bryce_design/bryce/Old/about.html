About

"Dishongh" is pronounced "dish-on." It's French. I'm not very French, though. I was raised in Houston and grew up reading a lot and drawing even more. I decided to pursue a master's degree in Technical Communication because it requires a keen eye for detail and I liked using pictures to communicate concepts. I graduated in 2007 and was quite grateful to have been immediately hired as a technical writer and graphic designer for a software company. After polishing my design skills, I decided I wanted to focus more on design and illustration, so I did that freelance for a year. But alas I could not turn down the opportunity to work fora truly great company (that's me doing the type on the white board)as a technical writer, so I did that for the next two years.

That pretty much brings us up to speed. On the side, I'm exercising my design and illustration skills to make fundog portraitsto help raise money for local rescue groups. Not on the side, I'm looking to be a part of an organization where I can make gnarly images all day long.
